# Privacy Policy - BinderVault

Last updated: 2026-02-13

## 1. Data Controller
Controller: BinderVault app publisher (contact: **Navalik78@gmail.com**).

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to the BinderVault mobile app.

## 3. Data We Process
Depending on features you use, we may process:
- Account data (Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication): name, email, profile image, user identifier.
- App and collection data you create: collections, card entries, preferences, scan usage counters, local settings.
- Purchase data: subscription/purchase status and product identifiers via Google Play Billing.
- Technical/network data required to call third-party APIs (for example Scryfall card lookup).

## 4. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)
We process data for the following purposes:
- Account sign-in and session management: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
- Core app functionality (collection, scan, app settings): performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
- Purchase verification and premium feature enablement: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) and legal obligations for accounting/tax where applicable (Art. 6(1)(c)).
- Security, fraud prevention, and abuse control: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).

## 5. Permissions
- Camera: requested only when you choose card scanning.

Camera frames are processed on-device for OCR. The app may send card lookup requests to Scryfall APIs when needed to identify cards.

## 6. Data Storage and Retention
- Collection and app data is primarily stored locally on your device.
- Authentication data is managed by Google/Firebase when you use Google login.
- Purchase records are managed by Google Play.
- Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- Data held by third parties is retained under their own policies.

## 7. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data.
We may share data only with processors/service providers necessary to operate the app, including:
- Google Sign-In
- Firebase Authentication (Google)
- Google Play Billing
- Scryfall API

We may also disclose data if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

## 8. International Transfers
Third-party providers may process data outside your country (including outside the EEA). Those providers are responsible for their transfer safeguards under applicable law.

## 9. Your Rights (EEA/UK where applicable)
Subject to legal limits, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and data portability, plus the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

To exercise rights related to app-controlled data, contact: **Navalik78@gmail.com**.
For data controlled by Google services, you can also use your Google account privacy controls.

## 10. Children's Privacy
BinderVault is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 11. Google Play Data Safety Summary
High-level mapping for store disclosure:
- Data collected: account identifiers (when signed in), purchase status, app activity/settings required for features.
- Data sharing: only with service providers required for app operation.
- Data security: data is handled through platform/provider security controls; local data remains on-device unless shared via enabled third-party features.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Updates are posted on this page with a revised date.

## 13. Contact
Privacy contact: **Navalik78@gmail.com**
